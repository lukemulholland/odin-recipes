Description

This is my favouite lasagne recipe, perfect for a cool winters evening. Pair it with your favourite glass of red wine for a relaxed weekend meal.

Ingredients

  • Lean beef
  • Minced onion
  • Crushed tomatos
  • Garlic
  • Lasagne sheets
  • Fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Cook the beef with the tomato, garlic and crushed tomato
  2. Place in oven proof dish and cover with a layer of lasagne sheets
  3. Repeat above until you have four to five layers
  4. Head oven to 250°C
  5. Place into the oven and cook for 40 minutes
