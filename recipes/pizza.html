Description

Pizza can be tricky to master but an absolute crowd pleaser. Follow these easy steps to impress everyone at your next dinner party

Ingredients

    For the dough:
  • 500 grams (about 4 cups) all-purpose flour (or 00 flour, preferred for pizza dough)
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 300 ml (1 ¼ cup) warm water
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
    For the toppings:
  • (340g) Can crushed tomatoes
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 200 grams (about 7 ounces) shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

    Making the dough:
  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center.
  2. In a separate bowl, activate the yeast by dissolving it in warm water with a pinch of sugar. Let it sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
  3. Pour the yeast mixture and olive oil into the well in the flour mixture. Gradually combine the wet and dry ingredients with a spoon or your hands until a shaggy dough forms.
  4. Transfer the dough to a lightly floured surface and knead for 10-15 minutes, or until the dough becomes smooth and elastic. Add a little more flour if the dough feels too sticky.
  5. Form the dough into a ball, place it in a lightly oiled bowl, and cover it with plastic wrap. Let the dough rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours, or until doubled in size.
    Preparing the pizza:
  1. Preheat your oven to its highest setting (ideally around 450°C or 850°F) for at least 30 minutes, with a pizza stone or baking sheet placed inside to preheat as well.
  2. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the dough into a 12-inch circle. You can use a rolling pin or your hands to stretch the dough.
  3. Transfer the dough round to the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. In a small bowl, combine the crushed tomatoes with a drizzle of olive oil, salt, and pepper to taste. Spread the sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a 1-inch border around the edge.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce. Add any other desired toppings.
  6. Bake the pizza for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool slightly before slicing and serving. Garnish with fresh basil leaves, if using.
